About Me

I am a simple man who enjoys the simpler things in life, listening to a good album, laughing at a good comedy, or even a simple stroll through the park. But oftentimes I find that the simple does not fully satisfy. The simple gets stale astoundingly fast. The human brain has an incessant appetite; it aches for something more, something a bit more purposeful, a bit more intricate; something a bit more challenging.

That is the duality that defines my being. I am the simple man who craves complexity.
