2048

A clone of the popular puzzle game originally made by Gabriele Cirulli. This clone contains all the features from the original with few new ones of its own.

Game Modes

Solo

Play the classic 2048 game. You only have four main gestures: up, down, left, and right. Slide the numbered tiles and merge them into the biggest tile possible. Watch out! As the tiles move around new tiles pop-in at random locations.

VS. A.I.

A mode where you compete with the AI to get the better score within the time limit. Choose the AI difficulty from easy, medium or hard in the titlescreen.

Two Player

Grab a friend and see who can get the best score before the timer runs out.

Grid Size

4x4

Play with the 4x4 grid to experience a classic game of 2048.

5x5

Play with the 5x5 grid for some extra room.

6x6

Play with the 6x6 grid for a wacky experience.
